2.1   Required environment¶

  • Windows 7／ 8

3   Run the tutorial¶

Your current directory is assumed to be the home of the USB unless specified.

  1. Run .demo/BatchFiles/Choreonoid-GRobot.bat GUI for Choreonoid launches.

./demo/BatchFiles/Choreonoid-GRobot.bat

  1. On Choreonoid GUI, read an existing motion by the following.

(English ver software) “File” –> “Open” –> “Pose Sequence” –> Choose GR001SampleMotion1.pseq

(日本語版) “ファイル” –> “読み込み” –> “ポーズ列” –> Choose GR001SampleMotion1.pseq

If the steps above don’t let you see the file, it’s there:./demo/Choreonoid-1.1/share/projects/GR001SampleMotion1.pseq

  1. The motion in the selected file should appear on the pane on the left side of Choreonoid. Click it (see the image below).

  1. On “PoseRoll” pane on Choreonoid, move the cursor (thin vertical line on the timeline) twhere you’d like to add the motion. Say, at the end of the existing motion.

  2. Right click on the robot’s pane, select “Edit Mode”. This allows moving robot parts.

  3. Move which ever part on the robots however you want :)

  4. Click “Insert”／”Current Motion” on “PoseRoll” pane. The newly added porting will be highlighted in red.

  5. Do not forget to save.

  1. It’s your choice to close Choreonoid or not once the work is saved.
